The Evolution of Video Games

Welcome to the journey of the development of video games

Video games have evolved from simple arcade games into a global industry that influences widely and shapes technology, entertainment, and culture. This website will explore the evolution of games over time, from the early pixelated game origins to the current 3D games.

Here you will discover the following content:

  • Game history: A review of the earliest games and how they laid the foundation.
  • Game eras: The key periods that defined the way games were played.
  • Design and impact: How games were made and their significance beyond entertainment.

Innovative Classic Games

  • "Pong" (1972) – The first widely known arcade game.
  • "Space Invaders" (1978) – Bringing video games into the realm of popular culture.
  • "Super Mario Bros." (1985) – Changed the landscape of home console games.
